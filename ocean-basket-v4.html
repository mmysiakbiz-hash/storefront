Our story

A fisherman's basket, scaled.

Ocean Basket began as one boat and a handful of men who had grown up fishing the reefs around Mahé. Twelve years on, our quay at Providence lands three tons of fish every morning — and every fish still passes through Seychellois hands.

We bleed and ice each catch within minutes of landing. We work to HACCP standards and the watchful eye of the US FDA. But the discipline is the easy part. The hard part — the part you can taste — is the care.

Lapeser pa zis en pri. I en lavi, en koutim — sa ki nou anvoy ou se sa ki nou manze nou menm.
Louis — Founder, Ocean Basket
