Our story

Thirty years
of sunrises.

Anse Kerlan Chicken Farm started in 1995 with a borrowed incubator and forty chicks behind the family house. Today the yard feeds Praslin — eighteen hundred layers, a broiler house, pigs, and one stubborn bull — and every egg still passes through the same family's hands.

We sell what the yard gives, the morning it gives it. No cold-store carousel, no middlemen, no mystery. If you want to see where your dinner grew up, come by — the gate is open every afternoon.
